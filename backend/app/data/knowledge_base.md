# Vijay Diagnostics — Knowledge Base

## About Vijay Diagnostics
Vijay Diagnostics is a multi-branch diagnostic and pathology lab chain offering
blood tests, imaging, and full-body health checkup packages. We are NABL
accredited, meaning our lab processes meet national quality testing standards.
Reports are reviewed by qualified pathologists before being released to patients.

## Home Sample Collection
We offer free home sample collection for blood tests within an 8 km radius of
any branch. Outside this radius, a convenience fee of ₹100 applies. Home
collection can be booked through our website, app, or by calling our helpline
at least 2 hours in advance. A trained phlebotomist will arrive within the
1-hour slot you select. Home collection is available from 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM
daily. Please note: tests requiring same-day fasting (like Lipid Profile or
Fasting Blood Sugar) must be booked for a morning slot, since you need to fast
for 8-10 hours beforehand.

## Test Preparation and Fasting Instructions
Different tests require different preparation:
- **Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS) and Lipid Profile**: Fast for 8-10 hours
  (water is allowed). Best done first thing in the morning.
- **HbA1c**: No fasting required, can be done any time of day.
- **Thyroid Profile (T3, T4, TSH)**: No fasting required.
- **Vitamin D and Vitamin B12**: No fasting required.
- **Liver Function Test (LFT)**: 8 hours fasting recommended for accurate
  triglyceride-related markers, though not strictly mandatory for liver
  enzymes alone.
- **Kidney Function Test (KFT)**: No fasting required, but avoid heavy
  protein meals 24 hours prior for the most accurate creatinine reading.
- **Urine Routine Examination**: Collect a mid-stream morning sample in the
  sterile container provided.
If you're unsure whether your specific test needs fasting, our booking
confirmation message always lists the prep instructions for each test in
your order.

## Report Delivery Timelines
Routine blood tests (CBC, FBS, Lipid Profile, LFT, KFT, Urine Routine) are
typically ready within 24 hours of sample collection. Thyroid Profile and
HbA1c reports take up to 24-48 hours. Vitamin D and Vitamin B12 reports may
take 48-72 hours since they are processed at our central reference lab.
X-Ray and ECG reports are usually available within 2-4 hours on the same day.
Reports are sent via WhatsApp and email automatically once ready, and are
also available for download by logging into our patient portal with your
registered mobile number.

## Safety and Hygiene Protocols
All phlebotomists use single-use, sterile needles and follow standard
infection-control protocols. Sample collection areas at branches are
sanitized between patients. We follow NABL-mandated quality checks at every
stage from sample collection to report generation.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Can I get my reports corrected if I think there's an error?**
Yes, contact our helpline within 48 hours of receiving the report. We will
investigate and, if needed, re-run the test at no extra cost.

**Do you offer corporate health checkup camps?**
Yes, we organize on-site health checkup camps for companies with 20+
employees. Contact our corporate sales team for package pricing.

**Can I reschedule a home collection appointment?**
Yes, you can reschedule up to 2 hours before your scheduled slot through the
app or by calling the helpline, at no extra charge.

**Is fasting strictly required for Lipid Profile?**
We recommend 8-10 hours of fasting for the most accurate triglyceride
reading, though modern guidelines consider non-fasting lipid profiles
acceptable for general cholesterol screening. Your doctor's specific
recommendation should be followed if provided.
